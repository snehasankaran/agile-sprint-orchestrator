## [Bug] Climate System Settings Not Consistent Across Vehicle Zones

**Status:** In Progress
**Priority:** High
**Assignee:** 

**Steps to Reproduce:**
- Start the vehicle and access the climate control system.
- Set different temperature values for driver and passenger zones.
- Observe the climate system behavior in each zone.
- Adjust fan speed or mode in one zone and monitor changes in other zones.
Actual:
- Temperature, fan speed, or mode changes in one zone sometimes affect other zones unexpectedly.
- Climate settings are not maintained independently across zones.
Expected:
- Each zone’s climate settings (temperature, fan speed, mode) should operate independently and not affect other zones.
